FAQ

Answers for some common questions.

Why web design and development?

I love it! That's why.

The ability to create a website, a brand, an app out of thin air is magical.

Building the vision and feel from an idea is the most rewarding and enjoyable process for me. Especially when everything comes together at the end.

How about frontend frameworks?

Currently improving my React knowledge.

Although most of the projects I work on don't directly benefit from frameworks, there is a lot to be gained under the right conditions.

Can you create a brand identity?

Short answer - Yes.

I've been tinkering with branding and identity for over a decade. From full on branding and identity kits to just a logo or a business card (yes, those still exist). I've done it all and I like it a lot.

Why the color picker and monospace type?

I wanted to add some sort of a customization. A UX choice if you will. Picking your favorite color for example. Hopefully it will be useful for visually impaired viewers too.

Some studies suggests that monospace fonts can helps dyslexic readers. It helps me. Let me know if it works for you. Oh, and I just like it.

What data are you collecting?

None. This website is entirely open sourced and hosted on GitHub. Check the repository here.

I have no way of knowing who you are, are you really here (are you?) or any other sort of information. There are no forms or any other way to input and send any information.
